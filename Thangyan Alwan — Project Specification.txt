Thangyan Alwan — Project Specification
1. Project Overview

Thangyan Alwan is a high-performance web application that recreates the nostalgic feeling of the Myanmar Water Festival (Thingyan). The application focuses on audio nostalgia, cultural memories, and traditional knowledge through a modern and elegant interface.

The app must be fast, lightweight, responsive, and emotionally engaging, delivering a smooth digital experience inspired by traditional Thingyan celebrations.
